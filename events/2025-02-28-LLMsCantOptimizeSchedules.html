Challenge 2:
Problem Size

The set of all possible configurations of given input

We have 10 employees , each with their own skills, availability, preferences, etc.

We schedule 7 days, there are 3 shifts per day and we need on average 3 employees per shift, potentially with different skills.

How many different configurations can we create?

10^63 combinations (7 days, 3 shifts per day, on average 3 employees per shift)

10^(7*3*3) or 10^63 configurations
