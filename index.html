Navigation Instructions

Most of these slide decks are 2-dimensional, where different chapters/subjects are different columns. Since this is different than most slide software, here are some basic instructions.

  • Press space to go to the next slide.
  • Press left/right/up/down to navigate through the deck on your own.
  • Press escape to go to the slide overview.
  • Some decks have presenter notes. Press s to show presenter view.

PDF of a Presentation

  • Open a presentation.
  • Add ?print-pdf to the URL.
  • In Google Chrome, print it to a PDF.
